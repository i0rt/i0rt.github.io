# Vietnamese Quick Pickle

* 1 daikon radish, julienned
* 2 carrots, julienned
* 1 cup water
* 1/2 cup rice wine vinegar
* 1/4 cup sugar
* 1 tablespoon kosher salt

1. Stir boiling water, vinegar and sugar until sugar is dissolved
1. Divide daikon and carrots into sanitized canning jar
1. Combine in jars. Store in fridge for 20 minutes and up to a couple weeks
